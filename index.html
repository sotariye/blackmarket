About the event

Welcome to Black Market 2026 — Toronto's largest community pop-up marketplace featuring top black-owned brands across Canada and Africa. This summer, we are bringing together our most promising creators, makers, and innovators to connect with thousands of local shoppers.

Whether you specialize in handcrafted fashion, clean beauty, wellness, home decor, or gourmet foods, this is your flagship opportunity to build your audience, grow your brand, and showcase your work.

⚡ Update: Initial booth spaces are now fully sold out! Due to overwhelming interest, we are currently searching for a larger venue in Toronto to accommodate more vendor booths and higher foot traffic. Join the waitlist below to secure your spot in line.

Every Booth Pass Includes:

  • Free venue high-speed WiFi
  • Featured business listing in the SoPlugged Directory
  • Social media features reaching 15,000+ local buyers
