About the event

Welcome to Black Market 2026 — Toronto's largest community pop-up marketplace featuring 100 of the top black-owned brands in Canada and Africa. This summer, we are bringing together our most promising creators, makers, and innovators to connect with thousands of local shoppers.

Whether you specialize in handcrafted fashion, clean beauty, wellness, home decor, or gourmet foods, this is your flagship opportunity to build your audience, grow your brand, and showcase your work.

Registration fee is flat **$250 CAD** per booth. Spaces are limited and allocated based on review to ensure a diverse, balanced showcase of categories.

Every Booth Pass Includes:

  • 6ft rectangular table & chair
  • Free venue high-speed WiFi
  • Featured business listing in the SoPlugged Directory
  • Social media features reaching 15,000+ local buyers
